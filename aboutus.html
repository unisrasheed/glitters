About Us

Glitters Forever has emerged as an enterprising company, offering a diverse portfolio of Facility Management, Housekeeping Services, Import and Export and Satellite Communication Technology services through its sister company PT Glitters Forever International Business House (Indonesia)

Established in 2001 Glitters Forever Housekeeping Services Pvt Ltd has emerged as an enterprising company in the beautiful state of J&K,India offering Facility Management/Carpet Shampooing Upholstery Cleaning/Sanitisation & Fumigation/deep cleaning services & Outsourcing of Workmen force.

We are a professionally managed company and the management team includes experienced and qualified personnel drawn from the Facility management/IT/Satellite Communication /infrastructure/management industries with over 25 years of Organizational Experience.

Our highly qualified & dedicated team strength of over 1500 employees, allows us to provide turnkey solutions in diverse areas. Our services are based on developing relevant strategies, implementing improvements and ideas, and finally monitoring the services on a continuous basis.

We work in close coordination with our customers so that we can offer the best-suited solutions as per their specific needs. This enables our customers focus on their core areas of the business and help enhance their performance in key result areas to deliver success in accordance with their respective goals.
